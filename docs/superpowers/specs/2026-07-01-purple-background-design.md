# Purple Background Design

## Summary

Change the full-page background of the Tetris app from deep rose (`bg-pink-900`) to soft lavender (`bg-purple-200`).

## Change

**File:** `src/App.jsx`

Replace `bg-pink-900` with `bg-purple-200` on the outermost `div`. All other styles, components, and game logic are unchanged.

## Why `bg-purple-200`

Soft lavender provides a light, airy background that contrasts with the dark game board and panels.
